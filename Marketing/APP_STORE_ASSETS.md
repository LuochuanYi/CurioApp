## üìä Performance Tracking & Analytics

### App Store Analytics Setup

#### iOS App Store Connect Metrics
- **Impressions**: How many times app appears in search
- **Product Page Views**: Visitors to app store listing
- **Downloads**: Total installations from App Store
- **Conversion Rate**: Views to downloads percentage
- **Retention**: Day 1, 7, 30 user retention rates

#### Google Play Console Metrics
- **Store Listing Visits**: Traffic to app store page
- **Installers**: Unique users who installed app
- **Install Rate**: Visits to installs conversion
- **User Ratings**: Average rating and review sentiment
- **Crashes & ANRs**: Technical performance tracking

### ASO Performance Tracking

#### Keyword Rankings (Weekly Monitoring)
- Track top 20 keywords across both platforms
- Monitor competitor keyword performance
- Identify keyword opportunities and threats
- Adjust strategy based on ranking changes

#### Conversion Funnel Analysis
1. **Search Impression** ‚Üí **Product Page View** (Search Conversion)
2. **Product Page View** ‚Üí **Download** (Page Conversion)  
3. **Download** ‚Üí **First Open** (Install Quality)
4. **First Open** ‚Üí **Trial Signup** (Onboarding Success)
5. **Trial Signup** ‚Üí **Premium Purchase** (Conversion Success)

### Campaign Performance Metrics

#### Paid Advertising KPIs
- **Cost Per Install (CPI)**: Target $3.50, Maximum $5.00
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**: Minimum 3:1 ratio
- **Lifetime Value (LTV)**: Average $45 per customer
- **Payback Period**: Maximum 90 days

#### Organic Growth Tracking
- **Referral Downloads**: Word-of-mouth growth
- **Social Sharing**: User-generated promotion
- **Review Generation**: Organic review velocity
- **Brand Search Volume**: Direct brand awareness
