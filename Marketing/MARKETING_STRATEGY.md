## üí∞ Pricing Strategy

### Freemium Model

#### Free Tier: "CurioApp Explorer"
- **Content Access**: 3 stories, 2 songs, 10 learning activities
- **Languages**: English + 1 additional language
- **Features**: Basic translation, standard audio quality
- **Limitations**: Limited downloads, ads between activities
- **Conversion Goal**: 15% to premium within 30 days

#### Premium Tier: "CurioApp Family" - $9.99/month
- **Content Access**: Full library (11 stories, all songs, 60+ activities)
- **Languages**: All 6 languages with unlimited translation
- **Features**: Offline mode, high-quality audio, progress tracking
- **Family Sharing**: Up to 6 devices, multiple child profiles
- **Value Proposition**: Less than $0.33/day for unlimited learning

#### Annual Plan: "CurioApp Family Year" - $79.99/year
- **Savings**: 33% off monthly price ($119.88 ‚Üí $79.99)
- **Exclusive Features**: Early access to new content, priority support
- **Additional Value**: Educational resources for parents
