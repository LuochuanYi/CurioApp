## ðŸŽ¯ Success Criteria & Go/No-Go Decisions

### Week 2 Checkpoint: App Store Approval
**Go Criteria:**
- iOS and Android apps approved and live
- App store listings optimized and ranking for target keywords
- No critical technical issues or user experience problems
- All marketing assets reviewed and approved

**No-Go Triggers:**
- App rejection or critical technical issues
- Major user experience problems in beta testing
- Legal or compliance issues with content or privacy
- Significant competitive threats or market changes

### Week 6 Checkpoint: Launch Performance
**Go Criteria:**
- 5,000+ downloads in first 48 hours
- 4.5+ star rating with positive review sentiment
- Technical performance stable with <1% crash rate
- Media coverage and influencer campaign performing on target

**No-Go Triggers:**
- <2,000 downloads in first 48 hours
- <4.0 star rating or negative review trends
- Technical issues affecting user experience
- Negative media coverage or PR crisis

### Week 8 Checkpoint: Growth Trajectory
**Go Criteria:**
- 25,000+ total downloads achieved
- 10%+ free-to-premium conversion rate
- Positive unit economics with LTV > 3x CAC
- Strong user engagement and retention metrics

**Scale-Up Triggers:**
- Exceeding download targets by 25%
- Conversion rates above 15%
- Strong organic growth and word-of-mouth
- Media and influencer campaign over-performing
